PRIVACY POLICY

COLOR VACUUM · Effective 2026-05-21

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application COLOR VACUUM ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use it. We collect the minimum data needed to operate ads and respect your tracking preferences.

1. What we collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly. The App does not require an account.

To serve advertising, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect:

  • Device Advertising Identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant permission via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Generic device information (model, OS version, language, country)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (country/region level)
  • Ad interaction events (impressions, clicks)

If you decline the tracking prompt, you will still see ads but they will be non-personalized.

2. Data stored on your device

The App stores a small amount of gameplay progress locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults API. Specifically:

  • Your best score
  • Highest unlocked level
  • Stars earned per level
  • Last played level (for the "Continue" button)

This data never leaves your device. You can erase it at any time by deleting the App.

3. Permissions

The App uses haptic feedback (Apple's Taptic Engine) for in-game vibrations. It does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, photos, location, or any other sensitive permission.

4. Children

COLOR VACUUM is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. Because the App does not collect personal data, it is compliant with Apple's Kids guidelines and applicable children's privacy regulations (including COPPA in the United States).

5. Third-party services

The App integrates the following third-party SDKs:

  • Google AdMob — serves rewarded video and interstitial ads. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Game Center — leaderboards and achievements. Only the GameKit identifier is used; no profile data is sent to us.

You can disable personalized advertising at any time from Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your iPhone.

6. Changes to this policy

If we add features that require new data handling, this policy will be updated and the App's privacy declaration in the App Store will be updated accordingly. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective" date above.

7. Contact

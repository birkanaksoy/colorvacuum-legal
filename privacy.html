PRIVACY POLICY

COLOR VACUUM · Effective 2026-05-21

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application COLOR VACUUM ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use it. Your privacy is important to us — this policy is intentionally short because the App collects almost nothing.

1. What we collect

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The App does not require an account, does not contain trackers, and does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

2. Data stored on your device

The App stores a small amount of gameplay progress locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults API. Specifically:

  • Your best score
  • Highest unlocked level
  • Stars earned per level
  • Last played level (for the "Continue" button)

This data never leaves your device. You can erase it at any time by deleting the App.

3. Permissions

The App uses haptic feedback (Apple's Taptic Engine) for in-game vibrations. It does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, photos, location, or any other sensitive permission.

4. Children

COLOR VACUUM is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. Because the App does not collect personal data, it is compliant with Apple's Kids guidelines and applicable children's privacy regulations (including COPPA in the United States).

5. Third-party services

The current version of the App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, attribution, crash-reporting, or backend service.

6. Changes to this policy

If we add features that require new data handling, this policy will be updated and the App's privacy declaration in the App Store will be updated accordingly. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective" date above.

7. Contact

Questions or concerns about this policy can be sent to abirkan.aksoy@gmail.com.

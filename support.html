Common questions

The game crashes on launch — what should I do?
Force-quit the app and reopen it. If the issue persists, try restarting your device. If still broken, please email us with your iPhone model and iOS version.

I lost my progress. Can it be restored?
Progress (best score, stars, unlocked levels) is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app erases this data, and we are not able to recover it remotely.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?
No. The current version is free with no ads and no in-app purchases.

Does the app collect my data?
No. Read the full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I trigger FEVER MODE?
Absorb objects of the matching color to fill the CORE meter at the top of the screen. When it's full, tap the FEVER button on the right to unleash a screen-clearing absorption frenzy.
